# DIE — Decision Intelligence Engine
**Code:** `DIE` | **Position:** Skill 5

> *Make better decisions, faster.*

---

## What This Skill Does

Structures a messy decision into a clear recommendation — with options, trade-offs, a cognitive bias check, and a first move.

---

## When to Use

- You're stuck between options
- You're circling a choice without landing
- You need a structured recommendation on a messy situation

**Trigger phrases:** *"Help me decide / options / trade-offs / recommendation / should we do X or Y"*

---

## Output

- **Problem clarity frame** — what is actually being decided (often different from what was asked)
- **Options architecture** — the real set of choices, including the ones not being considered
- **Trade-off matrix** — options scored against the criteria that actually matter
- **Cognitive bias check** — flags anchoring, sunk cost, groupthink, and other traps
- **Final recommendation** — a clear position with the first move

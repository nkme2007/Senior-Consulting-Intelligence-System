# EBE — Execution Breakdown Engine
**Code:** `EBE` | **Position:** Skill 3

> *Turn strategy into action — no ambiguity.*

---

## What This Skill Does

Takes a strategy, decision, or initiative and converts it into a fully structured execution plan with ownership, dependencies, risks, and a launch readiness checklist.

---

## When to Use

- A strategy or decision exists but the execution path is unclear
- "Great idea... now what?"
- A plan exists but ownership and dependencies are muddy

**Trigger phrases:** *"Project plan / roadmap / who owns what / execution / how do we do this"*

---

## Output

- **Phased project plan** — sequenced workstreams with milestones
- **RACI ownership matrix** — Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed — per workstream
- **Dependency map** — what blocks what, and in what order
- **Risk register** — top risks with likelihood, impact, and mitigation
- **Launch readiness checklist** — the gate criteria before go-live

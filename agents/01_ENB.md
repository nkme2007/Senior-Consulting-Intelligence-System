# ENB — Executive Narrative Builder
**Code:** `ENB` | **Position:** Skill 1

> *Sell the decision in 60 seconds.*

---

## What This Skill Does

Transforms a proposal, idea, recommendation, or decision into a leadership-ready narrative that wins approval in the room — or before you even get there.

---

## When to Use

- You need to present a proposal to leadership, sponsors, or an exec committee
- You have a decision to communicate — not just explain
- You need someone to walk away knowing what to do, not just what you think

**Trigger phrases:** *"Sell this / pitch this / get buy-in / exec summary / I need something for leadership"*

---

## Output

- **3-line summary** — the whole story in three sentences
- **Structured narrative** — pyramid logic: recommendation → rationale → evidence
- **Risks + mitigation** — anticipate the objections before they're raised
- **Clear ask** — one precise thing the audience needs to do, say, or approve

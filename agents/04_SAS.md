# SAS — Stakeholder Alignment Simulator
**Code:** `SAS` | **Position:** Skill 4

> *Predict resistance before it happens.*

---

## What This Skill Does

Simulates the room before you walk into it — mapping every key stakeholder, surfacing their surface objection and hidden agenda, and building a response strategy for each.

---

## When to Use

- You're preparing for a meeting, pitch, or approval where resistance is likely
- Multiple stakeholders with different agendas are involved
- Something has failed before due to stakeholder dynamics

**Trigger phrases:** *"Stakeholder prep / resistance / who will push back / prep me for this meeting"*

---

## Output

- **Stakeholder simulation cards** — surface objection + hidden agenda + response strategy per person
- **Meeting strategy** — sequencing, framing, and tone recommendations
- **Objection playbook** — pre-built responses to the most likely challenges

# CIM — Competitive Intelligence Mapper
**Code:** `CIM` | **Position:** Skill 8

> *Know who you're fighting — and exactly how to win.*

---

## What This Skill Does

Maps the full competitive landscape, builds deep competitor profiles, and translates intelligence into strategic recommendations and a sales-ready battlecard.

---

## When to Use

- You need to understand the competitive landscape
- You need to benchmark against rivals or anticipate competitive moves
- You need to defend market position or build a differentiation strategy
- You need to arm a sales team with competitive intelligence

**Trigger phrases:** *"Competitors / differentiation / competitive analysis / moat / who are we fighting / where are we vulnerable"*

---

## Output

- **Competitive landscape map** — 4-tier classification (Leaders, Challengers, Niche, Emerging)
- **Competitor deep dive cards** — strengths, weaknesses, strategic intent, vulnerabilities
- **Head-to-head battlecard** — you vs. top competitor, by decision criterion
- **Differentiation & positioning analysis** — where you win, where you don't, and why
- **Threat assessment & vulnerability scan** — where you're exposed
- **Strategic recommendations** — 3 moves to make in the next 90 days
- **Sales battlecard** — objection handles and competitive proof points

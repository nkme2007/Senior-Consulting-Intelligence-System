# BCB — Business Case Builder
**Code:** `BCB` | **Position:** Skill 9

> *Turn a good idea into an approved investment.*

---

## What This Skill Does

Builds a CFO-ready financial argument for any investment — from cost justification to ROI modelling to the executive summary that gets sign-off.

---

## When to Use

- You need to justify spend or request budget
- You need to prove ROI to Finance, CFO, or exec committee
- You have a good idea but no financial argument yet

**Trigger phrases:** *"Business case / ROI / budget / justify spend / CFO / payback / the numbers"*

---

## Output

- **Investment summary + 3-line CFO test** — can you pass it in 15 seconds?
- **Full financial model** — Total Cost of Investment (TCI) + benefit quantification + ROI
- **Scenario & sensitivity analysis** — best case, base case, worst case
- **Options assessment** — do nothing vs. alternatives vs. recommended
- **Risk register** — financial and non-financial risks with mitigations
- **Value realisation timeline** — when do the benefits actually land?
- **Recommendation & decision gate** — what you're asking for, and what happens next

# MKA — Metric & KPI Architect
**Code:** `MKA` | **Position:** Skill 2

> *Measure what actually matters.*

---

## What This Skill Does

Builds a complete measurement framework — from North Star to leading indicators — so teams know exactly what success looks like and how to track it.

---

## When to Use

- You need to define KPIs, OKRs, or success criteria
- A new initiative, product, or team needs a measurement framework
- Existing metrics feel disconnected from actual outcomes

**Trigger phrases:** *"KPIs / metrics / OKRs / how do we measure / North Star / define success"*

---

## Output

- **North Star Metric** — the one number that tells you if everything is working
- **Input vs. Output metrics** — leading and lagging indicators separated clearly
- **Leading Indicators** — early warning signals before outcomes materialise
- **Tracking Cadence** — who reviews what, when, and what they do with it
